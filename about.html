About

Erica K. Woods was born in Lorton, Virginia where she first discovered her passion for modeling at the age of 7. She later revived her love for modeling when she moved to Georgia to attend Clark Atlanta University, where she received her B.A. in Psychology in 2012. Erica K’s goals are driven through her inmost passions of modeling alongside her desires to help children of greater need who seek support within the community.

Erica K has won the 2014 V103 Car & Bike Show Model Contest, participated in a local commercial for Cala Bar and Grille as well a fashion segment for Atlanta & Co. on 11 Alive News, and has served as a corporate event model for the National Black MBA Conference. She also has a wealth of promotional modeling experience, having worked with brands such as Hennessy VS, Grand Marnier and Morgan & Morgan Law. Crossing over into the realm of spokes modeling and media correspondence, Erica K continues to push herself towards her goals and remains a force to be reckoned with in the modeling and entertainment industry.
